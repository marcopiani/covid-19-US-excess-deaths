States are ordered top to bottom based on the all-ages rate adjusted to the US age pyramid (last column). The numbers represent COVID-19 deaths reported per 1M population of the specific age band. The percentage in brakets show how that number compares to the US average for the same age category. The latter percentage also determines the color. Grey entries correspond to data suppression, e.g., for the sake of privacy.

Sources: 'Provisional_COVID-19_Deaths_by_Sex_and_Age' NCHS dataset (data as of +up and census table 'SC-EST2020-AGESEX-CIV'.
